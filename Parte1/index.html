Tribute to Steve Jobs

"The people who are crazy enough to think they can change the world are the ones who do."

TIMELINE

  • Feb 24, 1995 Steven Paul Jobs is born in San Francisco to Joannie Carole Schieble and Abdulfattah Jandali. The then-unmarried couple give up their son to adoptation. Paul and Clara Jobs become Jobs' non-biological parents.
  • 1961 The Jobs family moves to Mountain View, Calif., part of what would later become known as Silicon Valley
  • August 9, 2011 Apple Shares edge past those of Exxon Mobil to become the most valuable U.S. Company, in terms of market capitalization at $333.17 billion
  • August 24, 2011 Resigns from Apple CEO post; becomes chairman
